Summary

Iowa State University hosted a Money Game Jam in the Spring of 2014, where teams were tasked with creating a game that would encourage college students to save more and reduce their student debt.

My team decided to take inspiration from popular time-wasting apps based on consistently collecting points (see, Cookie Clicker). The user enters their bugeted earnings and spending over time, and the app would keep track of their overall buget.

In 24 hours, we weren't able to completely finish the project, but our idea managed to win third place overall.
