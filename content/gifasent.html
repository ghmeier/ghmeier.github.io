gifasent

Quick Facts

Stack: AngularJS, Firebase, Parse, Ionic, giphy

Time frame: Fall 2014

Collaborators: Luke Geiken

Final Product: Android and iOS

Source Code: gifasent webapp

Summary

Gifasent is a GIF messaging app created at Startup Weekend Des Moines in October 2015. The prototype, created over the weekend, won the event and we continued with the idea through January when we released a complete version. At its peak we had around 300 monthly active users, and 700 downloads across Android and iOS.

Being my first forray into app development as well as AngularJS, I'm super proud of the final product and use it intermittently today.
