Summary

A contract with the University of Colorado, Colorado Springs, I helped create this radio streaming app. At Square Infinity we used an SDK called Marmalade that allowed us to develop simultaneously for Android and iOS.

On this project I focused mostly on displaying and reading an rss feed that shows data from the UCCS Radio blog.

Right now it is only available on the Google Play store, so if you have an Android device, downlaod the app, and listen to the jams of Colorado!
