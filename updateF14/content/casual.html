Casual.

Outside of school and work, I absolutely love to create.

Whether it's starting a new coding project to add to the mass of unfinished work on my hard drive, drawing a Montanan skyline, filling in a fictional world, or working to better the community, I've always had a passion for dreaming up the impossible.

A lot of the things I try to create fall to the wayside along the way, so why bother you ask? I'll let a quote from one of my favorite books, Cloud Atlas speak for me.

"What is an ocean, but a multitude of drops?"

I'm a big fan of trying to make a difference with the things that I create, and to have a great success, there must be unsuccessful stops along the way. So, I'm off to build something big, hopefully something that can affect others.
