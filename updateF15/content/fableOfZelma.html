The Fable of Zelma

Research Presentation

Quick Facts

Language: C++

Time frame: January 2014 - September 2014

Collaborators: Professor Joseph Zambreno

Source Code: Fable of Zelma

Summary

Every year Iowa State hosts something called the IT Olympics on its campus in the Spring. This event covers areas from robotics to basic programming. These events are designed to help middle and high school students get a taste of both hardware and software.

As a part of this, Professor Zambreno has worked in the past to create scripting engines used to control a video game. There have been a few previous iterations, and Fable of Zelma is the version slated to appear this coming Spring.

I began work as part of an undergraduate team in the Spring 2014 semester, but when the rest of the team became busy over the summer, I took over as the sole developer.

Throughout the project, I've learned a great deal about OpenGL graphics, string parsing, and C++ conventions.
